INTRODUCTION

Join me on my journey as I delve into the world of fullstack development as a RevoU student. A Fullstack Engineer is a versatile developer skilled in both frontend and backend technologies. They are adept at creating seamless user experiences and robust server-side logic, ensuring efficient and scalable web applications. RevoU students master modern frameworks, databases, and development tools, preparing them to tackle complex projects and contribute effectively to any tech team. This study journal captures my weekly progress, challenges, and achievements as I learn to build comprehensive web applications from scratch
